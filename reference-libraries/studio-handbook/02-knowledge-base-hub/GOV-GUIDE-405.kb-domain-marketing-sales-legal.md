# KB: Marketing, Sales, and Legal

**Version:** 1.0.0
**Last Updated:** 2025-06-13
**Status:** Approved
**Knowledge Guardian:** @Charles (GCT-MKT-MMGR-001), @Delphine (GCT-MKT-SBDM-001), @Henri (GCT-LEG-LCOUN-001)

## 1. Purpose and Scope

This knowledge base article consolidates key policies, strategies, and guidelines across **Marketing, Sales, and Legal** domains within GenCr-ft Studio. It serves as a central resource for ensuring effective brand awareness, successful business development, robust intellectual property protection, and comprehensive legal compliance for all studio operations and products.

The scope of this document includes:

- Overarching marketing strategies, branding guidelines, and external communication protocols.
- Principles for business development, sales strategies, and partnership management.
- Core policies for intellectual property management, including copyrights, trademarks, and patents.
- Guidelines for legal compliance, data privacy, and contract management.
- Integration points with product development, financial management, and studio operations.

This document serves as a foundational reference for the Marketing, Sales, and Legal Teams, as well as Management, Product, and external-facing Gems.

## 2. Key Information / Concepts / Procedures

### 2.1. Marketing Strategy and Branding

GenCr-ft's marketing efforts focus on building a strong brand identity and engaging with our target audience.

- **Marketing Strategy (SSoT: `gcp-aethel-docs-req/01_Product_Vision_Strategy/Marketing_Strategy.md`):** Defines overall approach to market positioning, audience targeting, and campaign planning.
- **Brand Guidelines:** Comprehensive documentation outlining visual identity (logos, colors, typography) and messaging tone (SSoT: `gcs-core-governance/02-knowledge-base-hub/KB-Domain-Marketing-Sales-Legal/Marketing/Brand_Guidelines.md`).
- **External Communication (Protocol S11):** Governs all official communications to press, partners, and investors, ensuring consistency and strategic alignment.
- **Market Research:** Continuous analysis of market trends, competitor activities, and audience insights (leveraging `Iris`) to inform strategy.
- **Responsibilities (`Charles`):** `Charles` (GCT-MKT-MMGR-001) is responsible for developing and executing marketing strategies and managing branding.

### 2.2. Sales and Business Development

Strategic partnerships and effective sales approaches are crucial for GenCr-ft's growth and reach.

- **Business Development Strategy (SSoT: `gcs-core-governance/02-knowledge-base-hub/KB-Domain-Marketing-Sales-Legal/Sales-And-BizDev/BizDev_Strategy.md`):** Identifies and evaluates opportunities for expansion and partnerships.
- **Partnership Management:** Guidelines for identifying, negotiating, and managing relationships with distribution platforms, publishers, and co-marketing partners.
- **Monetization Strategy (SSoT: `https://github.com/GenCr-ft/gcp-aethel-docs-req/blob/main/01_Product_Vision_Strategy/Business_Model_Monetization.md`):** Defines how GenCr-ft generates revenue from its products (e.g., B2P, ethical microtransactions).
- **Sales Performance Analysis:** Tracking sales data and market trends to optimize revenue generation (collaborate with `Cresus`).
- **Responsibilities (`Delphine`):** `Delphine` (GCT-MKT-SBDM-001) is responsible for identifying and securing strategic business opportunities and partnerships.

### 2.3. Legal and Compliance

Robust legal frameworks and strict compliance ensure GenCr-ft operates ethically and securely.

- **Intellectual Property Management (Protocol S9):** Outlines procedures for identifying, protecting (copyrights, trademarks, patents), and managing GenCr-ft's IP, and respecting third-party IP.
- **OSS Compliance:** Adherence to Open Source Software (OSS) licenses, managed by `Léo` (GCT-UTL-OSLCS-001).
- **Data Privacy (SSoT: `gcs-core-governance/02-knowledge-base-hub/kb-domain-security/information-classification-and-handling-policy.md`):** Policies for handling sensitive data (e.g., GDPR compliance, player data protection).
- **Contract Management:** Guidelines for drafting, reviewing, and managing all legal agreements (e.g., vendor contracts, partnership deals).
- **Regulatory Compliance:** Adherence to industry-specific regulations (e.g., age ratings like PEGI/ESRB) and advertising laws.
- **Grievance Reporting (Protocol S18):** Procedures for reporting and resolving grievances, including Code of Conduct violations.
- **Responsibilities (`Henri`):** `Henri` (GCT-LEG-LCOUN-001) is responsible for providing legal counsel, ensuring compliance, and managing IP.

### 2.4. Integration Points and Cross-Functional Collaboration

Effective communication and collaboration are vital across these domains:

- **Product Team (`Béatrice`):** Marketing and Sales align with product vision and roadmap. Legal advises on product features (e.g., UGC, monetization).
- **Management (`Antoine`):** Oversees strategic alignment, budget allocation (Protocol S16), and ensures all activities are within studio objectives.
- **Studio Operations:** Legal ensures compliance of operational protocols (e.g., data handling in DevOps).
- **Art & Design Teams:** Marketing briefs for creative assets. Legal advises on IP implications of creations.
- **Finance (`Cresus`):** Sales and Marketing feed into financial forecasts. Legal advises on financial agreements.
